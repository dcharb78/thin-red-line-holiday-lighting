Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We install Christmas lights throughout Clarksville and Nashville, Tennessee, Bowling Green, Kentucky, and surrounding Middle Tennessee communities.

What's included in your packages?

All-inclusive packages cover commercial-grade materials, professional installation, seasonal maintenance, post-holiday removal after January 1, and off-season storage.

How do I get a free quote?

Text 925-895-4443 or 270-604-5265 with a photo of your home and a brief description of your vision. We'll provide a complimentary customized estimate with multiple options.

How accurate is the online estimator?

The tool uses OpenStreetMap building footprints when available, or manual square footage. It produces a ballpark range only — trees, dormers, and design complexity can change the final quote. Contact us for a precise, free estimate.

Do you install permanent lighting?

Yes. In addition to seasonal Christmas lights, we offer permanent outdoor architectural lighting solutions.
